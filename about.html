What is this?

A simple way to get a rudimentary network of the scientific collaboration between investigators in the biomedical and life sciences field based on their common publications.

How does it work?

Two scientists are considered connected if they have authored a paper together and explicit networks of such connections are constructed by using data drawn from the NCBI Pubmed database maintained by the Entrez query and database system at the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI). Because the Pubmed database only records biomedical and life sciences journal articles, the networks that can be constructed are limited to scientist having authoring papers in those fields.
